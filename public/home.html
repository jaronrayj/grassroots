Our Mission

Here at Grassroots, it is our mission to bring out the best of your communities. By signing up, you'll have access to a range of working community projects accross the world! From community engagement, environmental awareness, activism, and many more categories, Grassroots is your go-to app for organizing your community projects. Simply search for an existing project, join a project, copy a project's framework to implement it into your community, or create your own!
